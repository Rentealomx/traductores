Hélène Duval’s makeup must-have

Go-getter, active Parisian, yoga teacher and founder of yogawear company Yuj, Hélène, shares her absolute makeup must-have.

“Without a moment’s hesitation, it’s Lancôme’s Grandiôse mascara. Plain and simple, I can’t live without mascara. The proof? It’s impossible for me to start the day without a cup of coffee or mascara. When I don’t wear any, my eyes just don’t seem as bright, fresh and alive and I just seem more tired. This particular formula always promises a stunning, even layer across all lashes -- from root to tip. Simply stunning.”

Grandiôse, Lancôme.
