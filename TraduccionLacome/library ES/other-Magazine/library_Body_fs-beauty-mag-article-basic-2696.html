When cleansing isn’t quite enough after a late night

Your mother always told you never to go to bed in your makeup and she was right. Cleansing is key for removing pigments and impurities, but if you’ve had a late night – or several - it’s not quite enough. Accumulated fatigue considerably affects the skin’s self-defence systems and the knock-on effect is premature aging.

Night birds out after midnight should slather on Energie de Vie Masque Nuit by Lancôme, even if it’s just for a few hours, which will not only soothe partied-out – or simply under-rested – skin by lowering its temperature and creating a fresh sensation, but also giving it everything it needs to re-energize overnight.

It’s a dream in a bottle for those more difficult mornings, especially if you pair it with Energie de Vie Soin Liquide on waking, for superfood-based actives that pack a skincare punch and help melt away signs of fatigue.

Fresher, brighter, dewier: your skin will look completely rested and ready to get up and go, even if you’re not.

Energie de Vie Masque Nuit and Soin Liquide, Lancôme.
