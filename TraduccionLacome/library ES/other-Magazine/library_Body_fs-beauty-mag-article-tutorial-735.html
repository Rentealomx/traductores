1960s icons: Lisa Eldridge’s latest inspiration

Daring and audacious as ever, Lisa Eldridge is setting her sights on the eyes, this time with an up-to-the-minute take on the classic 1960’s ‘babydoll’ look. Having lined the upper lash line with a bold, graphic and pop-like line, Lisa goes for strong, fluttering lashes with the help of Hypnôse Doll Eyes. Pair with slightly rosy-blushed cheeks and a natural lip for a perfectly balanced look.
