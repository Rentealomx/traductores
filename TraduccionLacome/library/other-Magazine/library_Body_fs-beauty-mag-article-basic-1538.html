Beauty at 30: Time to create a winter beauty routine?

Beauty at 30: Time to create a winter beauty routine? Winter, it gives cheeks a rosy glow, contracts pores and tightens skin. And that’s all without considering goosebumps, which work to give skin a mini workout - no gym necessary!

Of course, cold weather climes aren’t just filled with beauty bonuses. After all, the biting cold of winter can also attack and irritate the skin causing it to dry out, lose glow and decrease in softness.

And, because this trend only compounds at age 30 as the skin gradually decreases in strength, there’s no better time to switch up your winter beauty routine with these simple essentials:

? In the morning, slip a small dose of Nutrix Royal, Crème Réparatrice Relipidante Intense by Lancôme on top of your everyday serum to create a protective blanket that wards off the effects of wind, rain and the cold, leaving skin looking serene, glowing and protected throughout the day.

? In the evening, turn to Nutrix Royal, Crème Réparatrice Relipidante Intense from Lancôme, but this time, one hour before bed, smooth it on in a thick mask-like layer that covers the face. The result will better prepare the skin for the chills of coming day.
