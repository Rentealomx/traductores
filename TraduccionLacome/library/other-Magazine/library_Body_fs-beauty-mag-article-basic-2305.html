Makeup no-go: Applying eyeliner to the wetline

“While eyeliner applied to the wetline can make you look mysterious and sexy, peskily enough, it also has a habit of making eyes often look smaller too.

A no-go then if you have heavy lids or small eyes. Instead, go for a kohl pencil that will accentuate your look like Lancôme’s Crayon Khôl. Simply dot on across the root of the lash instead of the wetline for a sensual, smokey look that makes eyes look bolder without changing their shape or structure.”
