Re-discover Lancôme’s Grandiôse mascara

Grandiôse, your go-to mascara for neverending lashes

Creating eye-catching curves from root to tip, Lancôme’s Grandiôse mascara isn’t just about curl. Think: amazing shape and a captivating look in more ways than just one.

Time to get addicted with these top five reasons to give Grandiôse a try:

What’s the secret behind Grandiôse’s length-defying lash? Forget one, there are in fact three secrets behind Grandiôse’s incredible wide-angle look.

1/ Its swan neck-shaped wand that’s been especially created to mimic makeup artists’ habit of turning and curling mascara brushes to achieve greater precision -- even on the absolute smallest of lashes. 2/Its elastomer flower-petal-like wand with bristles that work to separate and evenly coat lashes.

3/ Its deep charcoal-toned pigmented formula that uses light-boosting polymers like mini lash-intensifying magnifying glasses.

And that’s not all!

4/Grandiôse mascara also boasts a satin-smooth rose oil-enriched formula that softens and beautifies lashes day after day.

5/Grandiôse also offers an unbeatable 24-hour hold.
