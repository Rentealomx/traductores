Think pink with “La Vie Est Belle”

What if happiness had its very own fragrance? At least that’s what Lancôme asked itself when creating its iconic ‘La Vie est Belle’ perfume. Bright with warm, energizing, bold and enveloping rose and caramel-sweet iris fragrance, this mood-boosting blend comes encased in a veritable crystal smile. And, since 2012, this ode to joy makes for the perfect way to fill up on feel-good vibes, especially as the temperatures start to decline and the frenetic pace of our busy work lives start to take over. Slip on your rose-tinted sunglasses!
