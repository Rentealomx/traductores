Color your way to rainbow lashes

Rainbow lashes caught your eye? If there was one lash trend that beat out all others this summer season, it has to be the craze for colorful rainbow lashes. Ace the trend with these pro tips for creating the look with black mascara and eyeshadow.

Step one: Start by creating a thick film-like base to lashes with several coats of Grandiôse Noir Mirifique mascara. With its clump-free formula, Grandiôse is ideal.

Step two: Don’t wait for the mascara to dry. Now’s the time to make the most of its sticky phase to apply your choice of colored pigments. Next, take a blend of Color Design Mono shadows (in #P205 Lagon Secret, #T0002 in Belle Etincelle and #P203 in Rose Perlée) and apply on top of your fresh mascara to create your very own rainbow effect. Three colors are enough to create a beautiful result.

Step three: Repeat across the lower lashline.

Color Design Mono's in #P205 Lagon Secret, #T0002 in Belle Etincelle and #P203 in Rose Perlée, Lancôme. Grandiôse Effet Cils Déployés, Lancôme.
