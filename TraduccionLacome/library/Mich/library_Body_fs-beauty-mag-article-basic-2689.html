Lisa Eldridge’s beauty tip: the bold lip.

Lancôme’s Creative Director of Makeup, Lisa Eldridge, shares her latest product picks, application tips and expert tricks.

“A bold lip always makes for a chic statement. Just always make sure you keep the eyes simple. All it takes is a little liner and mascara to keep all the focus on your lips. A beautifully coral pink shade such as Absolu Rouge in Carrousel #349 makes for a vibrant mood-booster and the perfect way to show a little personality. I also love Rose Noire #484 with its sensual dark plummy color. Both shades deliver luxuriously saturated color. Finally, don’t forget to add a hint of blush to liven up the skin and have fun!”

Lisa Eldridge, Lancôme Creative Director of Makeup
