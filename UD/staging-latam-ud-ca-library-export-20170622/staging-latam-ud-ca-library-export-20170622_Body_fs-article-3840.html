Natalie Off Duty does it again with a smoldering #NakedSmoky eye.

To steal this look, apply High to lid. Sweep Radar into crease. Using Whiskey, deepen outer corner and blend toward crease. Smudge Radar along lower lash line.

Prime your lashes with Subversion Lash Primer, top with several coats of Perversion Mascara and tie in the look with Naked Naked Flush and Sheer Ladyflower Revolution Lipstick.
