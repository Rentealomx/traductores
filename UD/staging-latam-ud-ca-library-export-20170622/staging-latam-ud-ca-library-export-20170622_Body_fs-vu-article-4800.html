Looking for that pop of color this Halloween but don’t have your costume? Don’t freak. Our last Halloween look is a super easy Cleopatra look.


Apply Minor Sin Eyeshadow Primer Potion to lid, crease, brow bone and lower lash line.

Using Blackout Eyeshadow, create a bold brow.

Using the Electric Palette, apply a mix of Gonzo and Thrash Pressed Pigment to lid.

Blend out crease and edges with Thrashed Pressed Pigment.

Using Perversion 24/7 Glide-On Eye Pencil, create a dramatic wing on upper lash line. Heavily line lower lash line and connect to upper lash line.

Line lower waterline with Legend 24/7 Waterline Eye Pencil.

Apply Distortion Heavy Metal Glitter Eyeliner over the Perversion wing.

Prime lashes with Subversion Lash Primer.

Top with several coats of Perversion Mascara.

Apply Fetish Afterglow 8-Hour Powder Blush.

For an Egyptian goddess glow, apply Lit Naked Illuminated to cheekbone, nose and forehead.

Fill in lip with Stark Naked 24/7 Glide-On Lip Pencil.

Top with Matte Stark Naked Matte Revolution Lipstick.

Happy Halloween, UDers!
