Natalie Off Duty does it again with a smoldering #NakedSmoky eye.

To steal this look, press Combust (from the Naked Smoky Palette) over the entire lid for an opaque effect. Highlight the brow bone with Thirteen. Heavily smudge Dagger under the lower lashes to create a smoky liner, extending out into a wing.

Prime your lashes with Subversion Lash Primer, top with several coats of Perversion Mascara and tie in the look with Rapture Afterglow 8-Hour Powder Blush and Sheer Shame Revolution Lipstick.
