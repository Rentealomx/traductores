Tonight’s Friday Night Fix has us channeling our inner 90’s girl. Artist in Residence, Amanda Rodriguez shows you how to create this killer look using ?Naked3.

Amanda has already prepped her brows and complexion.

Apply ANTI-AGING Eyeshadow Primer Potion to the lid and under the eye.

With a crease brush, blend LIMIT into your crease.

With a shadow brush, press FACTORY into the inner lid.

With a crease brush, layer DARKSIDE over LIMIT to create depth.

With a dampened shadow brush, press TRICK to the center of the lid.

With a shadow brush, apply BUZZ to the outter corner.

Apply 24/7 Eye Pencil in ROACH to the lower lash line.

With a blending brush, soften the edges of the liner with NOONER.

Using a shadow brush, press DUST to the inner corner of the eye to brighten.

Apply 24/7 Waterline Eye Pencil in PUSH to the lower waterline.

Apply? PERVERSION MASCARA to lashes.

Outline the lip with 24/7 Lip Pencil in ?NAKED2 and fill in the rest of the lip with NATIVE.

Apply Sheer Revolution lipstick in SHEER OBSESSED.
