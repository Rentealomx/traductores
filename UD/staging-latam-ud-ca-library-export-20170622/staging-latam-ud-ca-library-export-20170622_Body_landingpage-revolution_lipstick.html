PRIME AND DEFINE WITH 24/7 GLIDE-ON LIP PENCILS,
THEN APPLY REVOLUTION LIPSTICK IN THE MATCHING SHADE
Our creamy, long-lasting, waterproof liner glides on smoothly, keeps lips nourished
and grabs on to your lip color to help it last. And our shade pairing guide makes it
easy to find the right shade to go with your favorite Revolution Lipstick.
Line the outside of lips with 24/7 Glide-On Lip Pencil in Ozone (our clear shade) to fill in any fine lines and prevent feathering.
Fill in lips completely with 24/7 Glide-On Lip Pencil in a shade that matches the Revolution Lipstick shade you’ll be using. This will define lips, intensify color and provide even longer wear.
Apply Revolution Lipstick. The Pigment Infusion System™ gives our formula its creamy texture, intensely pigmented color and extended wear.
The result? A long-lasting lip look with insane color payoff and lots of shine—or, in other words, creamy, badass luxury.
