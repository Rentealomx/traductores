You’ve seen Perversion in L.A. and Paris, but we couldn’t leave out New York. We can’t think of anyone better to take us to the hottest local, undercover spots in the city than DJ Kiss. Check out her picks!

Fluer Du Mal Showroom

“I love the incredibly sexy lingerie and ready-to-wear from this brand by former creative director of Kiki Montparnasee, Jennifer Zuccarini. Her chic showroom is situated on a nondescript street in Soho up several flights of stairs so unless you know where you're going, you’ll definitely miss it.”

Economy Candy

“While NYC candy aficionado’s head to trendy spots like Dylan’s Candy Bar or the candy store at FAO Schwartz, when I need a candy fix I always go to Economy Candy - my secret spot on the Lower East Side. You would totally pass by right by this place if you weren’t looking for it, but what it lacks in decor it more than makes up for with it's exhaustive variety of candy from all parts of the world.”

Freeman’s Restaurant

Situated in unmarked entrance in an alley off of Bowery, this restaurant is still one of NYC’s best kept secrets. Freeman's specializes in delicious ‘Rustic American Cuisine’ and has amazing reviews from diners lucky enough to have heard of it AND find it!

69 Chinese Restaurant

“Although at first glance, this place looks like just another a hole in the wall, it’s the hands down best place to go to get amazing after-the-club Chinese food. Located deep in the heart of Chinatown, only true New Yorkers know to go here for a late night chow down.”
