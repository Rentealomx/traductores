We teamed up with some of our favorite beauty bloggers to see why they loved makeup and what make them a beauty junkie. We’re lovin’ these responses -

Stephanie Villa of SoothingSista:

“Whenever I want to be my baddest self, I throw on a dark lip! Makeup lets me play with color and style, and allows me to express my truest self no matter how I’m feeling today. It’s not about covering your flaws, it’s about showing the world who you are. Some days it’s a bold lip, and some days it’s a cat eye!”

Sophie Chang of Fashionista804:

“Makeup is my creative outlet and my look differs on a daily basis depending on my mood. I love how makeup has no boundaries, and I’m able to be a different character everyday. Makeup is an extension of your personality - It’s a form of art and I don’t know what my life would be without it."

Sazan Hendrix:

“There’s something powerful about a bold lip that makes me feel like I can take over the world. #ImABeautyJunkie because I love how makeup can highlight my favorite features instead of concealing them.”

Elle Fowler:

“For me, make-up is not about covering my flaws or impressing anyone - it’s about showing the world the real me. I love expressing myself through colors, new looks and different styles. Make up isn’t about covering up - it’s about showing up - and being 100% confident in your look.”

Tracy and Stephanie of Eleventh Gorgeous:

“You guys know by now that we love to play with makeup. It used to be so intimidating for us but now it’s one of our favorite creative outlets that brought us closer together as sisters. Makeup is a way to express ourselves and get creative - whether it’s a bright lip today or a bold brow tomorrow.”
