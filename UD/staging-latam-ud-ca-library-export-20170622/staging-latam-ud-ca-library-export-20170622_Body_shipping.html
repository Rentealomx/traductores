Shipping Pricing & Information

FREE SHIPPING

Get FREE Shipping on all merchandise orders $50 and over (excluding taxes and any applied discounts).

UrbanDecay.ca ships via Canada Post to Canadian addresses only. Canada Post does not deliver on Saturday or Sunday. Urban Decay does not accept orders being shipped to APO/FPO & P.O. boxes.

SAME-DAY SHIPPING

To benefit from our FREE same-day shipping service, simply place your order by 12PM ET for delivery on the next business day. Products eligible for free same-day shipping will be designated as such on their product pages and is available for a limited time only. Same-day shipping service depends upon inventory availability, order deadlines, and if your shipping address is within the eligible postal code for this service. Due to Air Safety regulations, Hazardous Materials (sprays, nail polish and products with high alcohol content) are not applicable to our 24h shipping service.

 

CHANGE SUBMITTED ORDERS

Urban Decay unfortunately cannot modify orders that have been already placed.

 

REPORT ORDER PROBLEM

If your order is missing a product, contains a damaged item, or other concern, please contact a Urban Decay Customer Reprensentative at 1.844.483.3229 or email us using our Contact Us Form. 

To expedite your request, please have your order confirmation number available. We will always do our best to accommodate your concern as efficiently as possible. 
 

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We regret that we do not ship outside Canada at this time for orders placed at UrbanDecay.ca. Please refer to our Store Locator for a directory of stores that offer Urban Decay, listed by country.
