We brought you Perversion in L.A. last week, and today we’re taking you on another undercover tour of one of our favorite cities. French blogger Lisa Gachet of Make My Lemonade takes us to her favorite secret spots in Paris to rock her #biggerblackerbadderlashes.

Moonshiner

A true speakeasy from the 20’s hidden behind a delicious pizzeria. You’re in for a time travel!

Le Sphinx Paris

“The Sphinx is a tattoo shop hidden in one of the many small streets of Paris. By appointment only, you need to be an insider to find it.”

The Dirty Dick Pub

“The Dirty Dick is my favorite spot in Paris. The bar is set in a old brothel and has kept the sign. Its sublime tiki atmosphere from the 50’s is perfect for a last drink."

The Montana Night Club
28 rue Saint Benoit, 75006 Paris

"Very select club from the 6th arrondissement of Paris. You can easily spot actresses and other celebrities. It’s a very beautiful venue - small, dark and sexy all at once."

Yasmine Eslami

"Fine lingerie creator, Yasmine, cut her teeth working at the Vivienne Westwood workshop as well as for numerous international magazines. She creates lingerie that is sexy but never crude. Absolutely desirable.”
