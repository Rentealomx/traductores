Still haven’t come up with your perfect Halloween costume? No worries, we’ve got you covered with 3 easy looks. This is the lazy girls’ guide to Halloween makeup.


First up is an easy zombie look.

Apply Naked Skin Weightless Complete Coverage Concealer in a shade three shades lighter than your normal shade under eye and around mouth.

Contour hollow of cheek and shade around mouth with Crave Eyeshadow from Naked Basics.

Apply Bittersweet Afterglow 8-Hour Powder Blush over the dark contour, around brow and around inner corner of eye to simulate bruising.

Using Naked2 Basics, sweep Primal Eyeshadow in crease and blend up toward brow bone.

Without priming the eye area (since messy fallout is useful in this look), apply Crave Eyeshadow to inner corner, down bridge of nose and up to brow. Also apply around one nostril and softly blend down toward the mouth.

Working from the nostril, apply Bad Blood 24/7 Glide-On Lip Pencil over Crave and blend down toward mouth.

Apply Brickhouse Revolution High-Color Lipsloss over the mixture of Crave and Bad Blood.

Line lower waterline with Street 24/7 Waterline Eye Pencil.

Happy Halloween, UDers!
