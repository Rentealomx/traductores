Winter is coming, UDers - That means we’re taking full advantage of all forms of winter white for today’s Friday Night Fix. Artist in Residence, Amanda Rodriguez shows you how to rock this full winter look using our 24/7 Eye Pencils.

Amanda has already prepped her eyes using Naked2 Basics.

Using 24/7 Eye Pencil in Yeyo, create a dramatic wing liner on the upper lash line.

Next, line 24/7 Eye Pencil in Mushroom along the lower lash line.

Then, line lower waterline with 24/7 Waterline Eye Pencil in Legend.

Top lashes with several coats of Perversion Mascara.

To finish the look, line and fill with 24/7 Lip Pencil in Blackmail then apply Matte Revolution Lipstick in Blackmail.
