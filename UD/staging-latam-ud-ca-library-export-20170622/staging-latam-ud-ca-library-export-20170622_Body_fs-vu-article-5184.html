Today’s Friday Night Fix has us feeling colorful – even more so than usual! Artist in Residence, Amanda Rodriguez shows you how to rock this cool cat eye look using our 24/7 Glide-On Eye Pencils.

First, apply ANTI-AGING Eyeshadow Primer Potion to lid and under the eye.

From the NAKED BASICS palette, mix Skimp and Stark under the brow bone.

Apply 24/7 Gide-On Eye Pencil in ELECTRIC to the middle of the lid, then apply FREAK from the middle to the inner corner and down to the lower lash line. Finally, create a cat eye wing using ABYSS.

Apply PERVERSION Mascara to top and bottom lashes.

Apply Revolution High-Color Lipgloss in KINKY to the lips.
