	<p class="book_style_paragraph"><b>Why? Because they’re designed to comfort the face with a protective cloak, </b>oils have fast become a crucial ingredient within many an evening beauty routine.</p>
	<p><b>However, what you may not realize</b> is that worn under day cream or mixed with a favorite moisturizer right as it’s applied,<b> </b>oils can also create a wonderful protective blanket for the skin. Think of them as a soft cover that work to nourish and protect the skin from everything the environment can throw at it -- all the while giving the skin some enchanting glow.</p>
	<p><b>To apply, best to go with several drops of Lancôme’s Absolue Precious Oil</b> along with a little <b>Rénergie</b> cream that will soften skin tissue and relax facial features, assuring a bright glow even as the temperatures plummet.</p>
	<p>Consider it the perfect combination for a fresh, glossy complexion despite gloomily cold climes.</p>
