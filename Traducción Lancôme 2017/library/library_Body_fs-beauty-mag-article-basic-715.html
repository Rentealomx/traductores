How to plump up tired, hollow-looking eyes

Dark, hollow-looking eyes can be ageing, so always make sure to hydrate the entire area even when applying makeup. The best option? Blend a hint of contouring cream like Lancôme’s Crème Yeux Hydra Zen Neurocalm on the back of your hand with an eye cream such as Lancôme’s Effacernes Longue Tenue. When combined together and applied by gently by tapping on with fingertips, the duo works to improve colour, plump the entire eye area and give the entire face a fresher appearance.
