Lip tint without lipstick texture

Lip tint without lipstick texture

While experts still often recommend blotting lips on a kleenex to remove excess lipstick there’s good news for feather-light lip color fans: Thanks to a hot new generation of super-soft Gloss in love moisturizing lip glosses, great color no longer has to equate with heavy lipstick texture.

Gloss in love’s stunning secret? A silky moisturizing formula which combines bold pigments and lip-nourishing moisturizers to create vibrantly colored, line-free glossy lips without stickiness. All you need do to increase the intensity of your chosen shade? Layer, and layer again until you reach your desired color. Better yet, no matter how many times you reapply, the satin-like effect doesn’t weigh down lips.
