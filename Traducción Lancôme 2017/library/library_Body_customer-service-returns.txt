1/ Please fill out the Return Merchandise section on the back of the invoice (enclosed in your original  order) and place it inside the return package.<br />
2/ Affix the return label provided on your packing slip to the outside of the return package.<br />
If the label is not available, please contact Customer Service toll free at 1-800-784-8722, Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time, excluding holidays, or contact us online.<br />
3/ Make a copy of the completed invoice and UPS tracking number for your records.<br />
4/ Your return shipping via UPS will take 3 to 5 business days to arrive at our facility. Upon receipt, it will be processed within 1 to 2 business days. Depending on your financial institution, it can take 5 to 7 business days to post to your account.<br />
