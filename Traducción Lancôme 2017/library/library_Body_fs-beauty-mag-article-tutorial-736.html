Bigger, Brighter Eyes

Bigger, Brighter Eyes

Eyes often say a lot more than words ever can, so make them bigger, brighter and much more expressive. Lisa Eldridge reveals her five key steps for reviving eyes. 1) Apply a droplet of Génifique Light-Pearl to help eradicate bags. 2) Brighten the wetline of the lower eyelid and darken the crease of the upper eyelid. 3) Then, lighten both contrasting areas with Crayon Miracle in ‘lumière défatiguante’. 4) Finish up by showcasing lashes by volumizing, curling, thickening them and separating them at the roots. 5) Blot a little coral colour onto lips and cheekbones to create a brightening optical illusion and you’ll also boost the white appeal of brighter eyes. What tips do you have for keeping eyes looking bright and powerful?
