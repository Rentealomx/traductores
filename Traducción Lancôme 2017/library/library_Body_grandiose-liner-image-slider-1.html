GRANDIÔSE
LINER STYLE

Draw a line along the upper eyelashes, making sure the thickness is equivalent to half of the upper eyelid. Create a line along the crease of the eyelid. Finish by drawing three downward lines from the roots of the lower eyelashes.

Draw a line along the upper eyelashes and another along the crease of the upper eyelid. Create a line along the lower eyelashes. Leave 2mm between the two lines at the outer corner of the eye. Draw a half moon shape at the outer corner. Add a twist by drawing a dot and a rectangle with a colored liner for a pop effect.

Draw a thick line along the upper eyelashes towards the outer corner of the eye. Add a line starting from the outer corner, following the crease of the eyelid. Underline the bottom of the eye by drawing a line along the lower eyelashes and continue towards the outside until it meets the first line.

Draw a line from the inner corner of the eye towards the out-side, ending in an upward direction. Start from this point and continue the line towards the inner corner of the eye, 3mm above the first line. Underline the bottom of the eye by drawing a line along the lower eyelashes. Create a second line 2mm below the lower eyelashes. To add a graphic touch, add a dot on the outside of the eye.
