	<p class="book_style_paragraph"><b>In Asian cultures, </b>water has always been central to women’s beauty and hygiene routines, leading Japanese and Korean beauty brands to create ‘cosmetic waters’, products with a much higher water content than usual that are more easily absorbed into the skin.</p>
	<p></p>
	<p><b>Lancôme has looked to this new beauty technology to create its own liquid cream</b>, resulting in a hybrid lotion-serum-day cream: <b>Énergie de Vie Soin Liquide</b>. Harnessing Liquid Care technology derived from cosmetic waters, it’s made up of a 24% hydrating agents – a market record - to quench the skin.</p>
	<p></p>
	<p><b>A special mention goes to the fluid, super fresh texture </b>that not only intensely re-energizes the skin, it also provides water-tight protection against whatever the day has to throw at you. In skin terms, it’s all the hydration power of an herbal tea, with the energy hit of a coffee.</p>
