	<p class="book_style_paragraph"><b>Extend summer’s glow long after your holidays have finished with Grandiôse’s three new gloriously enchanting lash colours. </b></p>
	<p>Showcasing a specialised 25° angled swan-like neck that has been designed to help bring mascara to each and every last lash, you could say that <b>Grandiôse</b> has reinvented mascara. So much so that in just one year it has scooped up an impressive twenty-odd product prizes. Now comes <b>Grandiôse’s</b> latest reinvention: three new colour picks, each more seductively beautiful than the next. Think Bleu Mirifique with a dark navy colour that evokes blue ocean beauty; Violet Mirifique, an indigo that takes colour inspiration from Provençal lavender fields and Saphir Mirifique, an electric shade of blue that makes for the perfect make-up partner for evoking hot summer nights out. Better yet, every colour is made with an enriching, softening and lash-densifying infusion of Lancôme’s signature native Rose cells and comes ready to be paired with a matching Art Liner. Whether you go for the full playful look or think relaxed for a slightly more unexpected yet beautiful final result, the choice is yours.</p>
