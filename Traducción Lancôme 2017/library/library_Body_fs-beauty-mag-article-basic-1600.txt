	<p class="book_style_paragraph"><b>Part innovative gem, part heritage lip color, Lancôme’s new lip oil, is set to blaze new trails in the beauty world -- and all for super sexy lips.</b></p>
	<p>The latest in <b>Lancôme</b> lip offerings is certainly set to spare us some frustrations. The concept? A well-executed new lipstick hybrid that fits somewhere between <b>Armand Petitjean’s </b>original 1946 <b>Shaker </b>and the ever-popular <b>Juicy Tubes</b>.</p>
	<p>It all starts with an innovative two-part oil-based formula that when shaken mixes rich pigments into one softly textured color. Add to that innovative cushion technology, which has been harnessed for the first time to create a soft, rounded lipstick-shaped foam tip to help control color. And, finally as a sign of the times, include a modern update of the <b>Shaker’s</b> original curves to keep in step with today’s tastes and what you get is an altogether surprising new must-have.</p>
	<p></p>
