It’s ideal for me because the biggest thing I dislike about makeup is taking it off. The most annoying part being mascara which I apply in several thick layers to create a bold look, and which always takes a long time to remove. My trick? I soak some cotton wool with Bi-Facil (after shaking) and I leave it to soak on the eyes for 10 minutes. Then, all I do is smooth the disk from top to bottom and the mascara melts away. It’s great. I’ll admit that it has become my best friend after nights out when I go out late and all I want to do is sink into bed when I get home.
