	<p class="book_style_paragraph"><b>Don’t: “</b>Apply blue shadow to the both upper and lower lids while also accentuating the entire look with blue mascara.”</p>
	<p><b>Do: </b>“Play with on-trend blue tones, but by following a few golden rules: 1/ Dust on a little shadow to the lower lid by creating a washed-out daytime version of a classic smoky eye. 2/Ditch the mascara! Or, if you can’t imagine going without mascara, opt for a nude option that simply highlights lashes, making sure blue’s endless playfulness is suitably accentuated.” Miky, <b>Lancôme</b> makeup artist.</p>
